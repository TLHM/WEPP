Welcome to WEPP!

Load a project folder with .json ERP files, then select peaks with your mouse! Use left click/drag to select positive peaks, and right click/drag to select negative peaks. You and click on peaks and delete them if you need to. Once you have peaks you like, hit the space bar to accept and continue on to the next bin or file. When you finish, you can click the export button to download your peaks as a csv!
