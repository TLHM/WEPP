Web ERP Peak Picker

Welcome to WEPP!

Load a project folder with .json ERP files, then select peaks with your mouse! For more details, see the github page.
  • Use left click/drag to select positive peaks, and right click/drag to select negative peaks.
  • You can click on peaks and delete them if you need to.
  • Once you have peaks you like, hit the space bar to accept and continue on to the next bin or file.
  • You can also adjust the y axis with your mouse.

When you finish, you can click the export button to download your peaks as a csv!
